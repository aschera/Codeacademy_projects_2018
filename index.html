Paris travel blog

Instructions

In this project, you will follow step-by-step instructions to produce a travel blog website on your own computer. There will be a few different sections on your page: a nav bar, images from your travels, highlights from the trip, the full text of the post, and your contact information at the end of the page. In order to complete this project, you must know how HTML code is structured, and a few basic HTML tags.
